Equitecture

Equitecture is a tad fintech startup based in Hyderabad, founded by a team of four passionate entrepreneurs with diversified expertise, with a mission to provide customised capital market research dashboards along with self-serve PMS solutions through mobile-centric design for our customers.

We believe that portfolio management, among the masses, is indispensable in post-pandemic life. As India emerged as the top economy (thanks to its assertive foreign and regional policies), so has the rise in demand for PMS solutions in the mid-income population.

Therefore, Equitecture’s vision is to "give you a pleasant portfolio management journey along with centralised investment tracker", and we hope that everyone can enjoy a positive and pleasant experience!
